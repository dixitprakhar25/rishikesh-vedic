Common Questions

Quick answers to frequently asked questions

How do I book a reading?

Click the "Book Reading" button in the navigation menu or visit our booking page. Select your preferred astrologer, choose a date/time, and complete payment.

What if I don't know my exact birth time?

Birth time is important for accuracy. Check your birth certificate or hospital records. If unavailable, contact us at support@rishikeshvedic.com and we'll guide you through options.

Can I reschedule my session?

Yes! Contact us at least 24 hours before your scheduled session to reschedule. We'll work with you to find a new time.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer full refunds if you cancel at least 48 hours before your session. Cancellations within 48 hours receive a 50% refund. No refunds for no-shows.

Is my information confidential?

Absolutely. All birth data and session content remain completely private and confidential.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and international payment methods through our secure payment processor.
