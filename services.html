Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from Western astrology?

Vedic astrology uses different zodiac calculations (sidereal vs. tropical) and incorporates sophisticated timing techniques. It's generally considered more predictive and detailed.

Do I need to believe in astrology for this to work?

No. Many clients come skeptical and are surprised by the accuracy. Approach with an open mind and see for yourself.

What if I don't know my exact birth time?

Birth time is important for accuracy. Check your birth certificate or hospital records. If unavailable, contact us‚Äîthere are techniques for approximate times.

Is the session really translated?

Yes! Every session includes professional translation. You'll hear everything the astrologer says in clear English.

How do I prepare for my session?

Come with an open mind and specific questions you'd like addressed. We'll send preparation guidelines upon booking.

Is my information confidential?

Absolutely. All birth data and session content remain completely private.
